Read the following situation and answer question 6

Camila’s fourth graders are going to practice their oral skills. She wants her students to describe location in a functional context, and so she will take advantage of the school field trip to the zoo they enjoyed the day before.

Question 6

Given her goal, which of the following strategies is more appropriate to carry out?

Correct answer: c — Explanation

La opción c es la más apropiada porque cumple los dos requisitos del objetivo: desarrollar habilidades orales en un contexto funcional. El roleplay de visitante y guía en el zoológico es una situación real y comunicativa donde describir ubicaciones tiene un propósito genuino. La opción a trabaja oralmente pero de forma controlada y mecánica (verdadero/falso). La opción b también es oral pero el foco es adivinar el animal, no describir ubicaciones funcionalmente. Solo la c usa el lenguaje de localización con un propósito comunicativo auténtico.

Read the following situation and answer question 7

Omar wants his students to develop their speaking skills. He has planned the following teaching sequence:

  • The teacher shows the students an emergency backpack and asks about its purpose and the kind of objects it should carry. Students discuss and share their answers.
  • Next, the teacher presents a situation on the board: “Lately, there have been frequent tremors in our country and we all know a strong earthquake may occur sooner or later. So, it’s important to have an emergency backpack in which you can carry necessary objects to survive in case of an earthquake.”
  • Then the teacher gives the following instructions: “In groups, make a list of the 10 most important objects to carry in your emergency backpack. Then present your list and explain why the objects you have chosen are important to carry.”
  • The students are given prompts such as “We consider that having a… is important because…” to use during the activity.
  • Each group presents their list and supports their choices using the prompts given.
  • Finally, the teacher asks the students to analyze the kind of language they used during the activity.

Question 7

Which of the following methods is Omar developing in his teaching sequence?

Correct answer: c — Explanation

La secuencia de Omar sigue exactamente la estructura del TBL: hay una tarea comunicativa concreta (hacer una lista de los 10 objetos más importantes y presentarla justificando las elecciones), los estudiantes usan el lenguaje de forma natural y con propósito real para completarla, y al final el profesor pide que analicen el lenguaje usado (lo que en TBL se llama language focus o post-task). No es Problem-Based Learning (a) porque el foco no es resolver un problema complejo de investigación sino completar una tarea oral. No es Content-Based Learning (b) porque el contenido temático es solo el vehículo para practicar el speaking, no el objetivo de aprendizaje en sí. (Willis, 1996)

Read the following situation and answer question 8

Rebeca wants to help her fifth grade students improve their speaking fluency through a functional activity.

Question 8

Taking her purpose into account, which of the following activities is it appropriate to carry out?

Correct answer: a — Explanation

El roleplay de una llamada telefónica entre secretaria y estudiante es una situación real y auténtica donde los estudiantes necesitan hablar de forma fluida y natural para lograr un propósito comunicativo genuino. La opción b también es oral pero es un dictado, que no desarrolla fluidez sino precisión en la escritura. La opción c trabaja la pronunciación y velocidad con un trabalenguas, lo cual puede ayudar con la pronunciación pero no es una actividad funcional: memorizar un trabalenguas no simula ninguna situación comunicativa real de la vida cotidiana. (Harmer, 2007)

Read the following situation and answer question 9

Tatiana’s students have drawn the timeline of a typical day in the life of their favorite artist, and they are now getting ready to present it orally. Taking advantage of that context, Tatiana would like her students to develop their oral fluency.

Question 9

Considering the teacher’s purpose, which set of instructions is it appropriate to deliver before the students give their oral presentations?

Correct answer: c — Explanation

Es la única instrucción que apunta directamente a desarrollar la fluidez oral: pide a los estudiantes sonar naturales, evitar pausas excesivas y cuidar la pronunciación, todos aspectos clave de la fluidez. La opción a se enfoca en corregir errores gramaticales, lo cual apunta a la precisión (accuracy), no a la fluidez. La opción b pide escribir y memorizar las ideas, lo cual convierte la presentación en una reproducción mecánica de texto escrito, que tampoco desarrolla fluidez oral genuina. (Thornbury, 2005)

Read the following situation and answer question 10

Carmen has planned the following teaching sequence to develop her third graders’ writing skills:

  • The teacher writes “Pollution is destroying our planet” and divides the board in two columns: “What do you know about the issue?” and “How could we solve this issue?”
  • The students work in groups to answer both questions. A member of each group writes some of the group’s ideas in each column.
  • Then the teacher asks the students to look for additional information at the school library and take notes of relevant ideas.
  • Back in the classroom, in groups, they choose the best ideas to save our planet. Finally, the teacher asks them to work on a short text to present their ideas to the class.

Question 10

Which of the following is NOT an objective developed in Carmen’s lesson?

Correct answer: a — Explanation

En ningún momento de la secuencia Carmen se enfoca en verificar la precisión gramatical de los estudiantes: no hay corrección de errores, no hay retroalimentación sobre la forma del lenguaje, ni ninguna actividad orientada a evaluar que escriban con exactitud gramatical. Las opciones b y c sí están presentes: el aprendizaje cooperativo aparece porque los estudiantes trabajan en grupos durante toda la secuencia, y el speaking aparece cuando los grupos presentan sus ideas oralmente a la clase. La secuencia desarrolla pensamiento crítico, colaboración y escritura con propósito comunicativo, pero checking accuracy no forma parte de ninguno de sus pasos.
